Flex Text Example

#text

  • The first item is fixed width which its #text child takes on and becomes responsive.
  • The second item is basis width which is also taken on by its #text child which then becomes responsive.
  • The third item has no width so it takes on the max-width of its #text child.
Fixed width item: It was the best of times, it was the worst of times, it was the age of wisdom, it was the age of foolishness...
Basis width item: it was the epoch of incredulity
No width item: it was the epoch of belief
